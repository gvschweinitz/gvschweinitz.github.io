Gregor von Schweinitz

Assistant Professor of Economics, esp. Quantitative Macroeconomics

Leipzig University

Halle Institute for Economic Research (IWH), Member of the Leibniz Association

Biography

I am Assistant Professor for Quantitative Macroeconomics at Leipzig University. and head of the research group “Volatility, Growth and Financial Crises” at the IWH. In my research, I analyze the link between financial crises and macroeconomic aggregates. In ongoing work, I focus on Bayesian inference in structural VAR models and the usage of large datasets to construct external instruments for shocks in empirical macroeconomic models.

Interests

  • Economic crises
  • Business cycle dynamics
  • Early-warning models
  • Structural identification in empirical macroeconomics
